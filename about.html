About Techmorphix Solutions

Innovative technology solutions driving business transformation since 2008

Our Story

Founded in 2008, Techmorphix Solutions began with a simple mission: to help businesses leverage technology to solve real-world problems. What started as a small team of passionate technologists has grown into a comprehensive IT services company with over 150 professionals serving clients across multiple industries.

Over the years, we've evolved our service offerings to address the changing technology landscape, but our core commitment to client success has remained unwavering. We believe that technology should be an enabler, not a barrier, and we work tirelessly to ensure our solutions create tangible business value.

Today, Techmorphix Solutions is recognized as a trusted technology partner for organizations ranging from innovative startups to established enterprises. Our team combines deep technical expertise with business acumen to deliver solutions that not only meet immediate needs but also support long-term strategic objectives.

Our Journey

2008

Company Founded

Techmorphix Solutions was established by Michael Davidson with a team of 5 developers, focusing on custom software solutions for small businesses.

2010

First Major Client

Secured our first enterprise client and expanded the team to 25 professionals, adding web development and IT consulting services.

2013

Cloud Services Division

Launched our cloud services division in response to growing client demand, becoming an AWS Consulting Partner.

2015

International Expansion

Opened our first international office in London, expanding our client base across Europe with a focus on financial services.

2018

Cybersecurity Practice

Established a dedicated cybersecurity practice led by David Rodriguez, achieving ISO 27001 certification for our security services.

2020

Remote Work Solutions

Rapidly developed and deployed remote work enablement solutions, helping clients navigate the global pandemic's challenges.

2023

AI & ML Services

Launched our AI and Machine Learning practice, helping clients leverage data-driven insights for business transformation.

Today

Continuing Innovation

Currently serving 200+ active clients across 15 countries with a team of 150+ technology professionals dedicated to driving digital excellence.
